## What to build

Show the user's email address as a read-only field on the profile editing form, so users can confirm which email is on their account without navigating elsewhere.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] `email` prop is passed from `ProfilePage` into `ProfileForm`
- [ ] A read-only input field displays the email address, styled as disabled/grayed out to indicate it is not editable
- [ ] A "Verified" badge (e.g. `CheckCircle2` icon or green text) appears beside the email field
- [ ] The field submits no data — it is display-only
- [ ] Type-check passes with zero new errors
